# Roadmap

Pre-1.0. Direction, not dated commitments.

## 0.1.x

- The evolutionary core (`darwin-core`): population, mutation, crossover, fitness.
- The `wickra-darwin` CLI.
- The ten-language binding surface over the C ABI hub.
- The golden corpus (fixed-seed searches, byte-identical across languages).
- The full CI matrix.

## Later

- Richer genetic operators and selection strategies.
- Multi-objective fitness (Sharpe / Calmar / drawdown fronts).
- Parallel island models.

Feedback via [issues](https://github.com/wickra-lib/wickra-darwin/issues).
